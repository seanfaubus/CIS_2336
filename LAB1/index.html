About Me

My name is Sean Faubus, I was born and raised in Bastrop, Tx. In 2014 I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a degree in biology. A couple years later I joined the United States Marine Corps. I served four years as a parachute rigger attached to Marine special forces on the North Carolina coast. I got out of the Marines and decided to switch up my career path. I am currently a Computer Information Systems student at the University of Houston. I will be graduating in December of 2023 looking to become a data analyst or a python programmer. I like to spend my free time outdoors. Whether that’s hunting, fishing, running, walking, or playing soccer, I’m happier outdoors. I’ve been married to my wife Catie for almost 5 years, and we have a beautiful two-year-old daughter Lucy. I am a huge Arsenal fan. One day I’ll make it to London for a game.

Enjoy this radical tune!

Goals

I want to make at least a 3.8 GPA each semester here at UH. Each summer I want to secure an internship. The short-term goal of most importance however is to secure a job before I graduate which has me performing data analytics or programming with python. Throughout all this, I want to balance being a husband, a father, and a student with as much care and attention as possible. After graduation, I want to find a great job that I thrive in as well as provides me a great work/life balance. My longest-term goal is to purchase a nice piece of Texas that my family and I can escape to whenever we like. Owning land has been a dream of mine for as long as I can remember. Having a piece of land that I can work on and make my own is what I look forward to most in life.
